CandidaMine, an integrative omics database for Candida yeast pathogens.
===============================
CandidaMine, comprehensively integrates information from various studies and resources to facilitate exploration, analysis, and interpretation of *Candida* related studies. It will be regularly updated and extended with new *Candida* species and datasets. It is aimed to serve as a dedicated resource for the broad research and clinical community. CandidaMine is powerby Intermine.
